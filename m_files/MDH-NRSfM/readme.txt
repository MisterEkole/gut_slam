MATLAB implementation of our CVPR'16 paper:  Inextensible Non-Rigid Shape-from-Motion by Second Order Cone Programming.
A. Chhatkuli, D. Pizarro, T. Collins and A. Bartoli

Requires the yalmip library and Mosek solver.

Test data made with KINECT Paper from [A.Varol, M. Salzmann, P. Fua and R. Urtasun, A Constrained Latent Variable Model. In CVPR 2012]
